Prixima is a highly flexible free Bootstrap template for the digital marketing business. It comes with a background image in the hero header area that makes your images and content stand out with a clean and minimal look. Prixima is 100% responsive and looks great on every device, whether a small smartphone screen or a large desktop screen. All the features and functions of this HTML5 template give it a sophisticated appearance. This responsive website template is an excellent option for anyone looking to establish a digital marketing business.

Free Bootstrap 5 HTML5 digital Marketing Website Template
Besides, it encompasses lots of tech-savvy features and assets like a hero header with slider, on hover effect, call to action button, testimonial carousels, social media linkup icons, footer navigation, and many more.Prixima can be a great option to begin your project.
